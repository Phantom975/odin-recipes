Tater Tot Casserole

Description

Tater tot casserole is a simple and fast meal for busy families. The biggest benefit this dish has is the versatility, so you can alter it as much as you need depending on what you have on hand. A large variety of vegetables can be used and a multitude of cream soups can be used to pull it together. Anybody who has a busy lifestyle should certainly keep ingredients on hand to be able to make this in a pinch.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb lean ground beef (the leaner the better)
  • 1/2 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 15 oz can of green beans, drained
  • 10.75 oz can condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 cups frozen tater tots
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375F.
  2. Lightly grease an 8 or 9-inch baking dish with cooking spray. Set Aside.
  3. Brown the ground beef in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Drain excess fat.
  4. Add the onions to the skillet and saut√© for 4 to 5 minutes until translucent.
  5. Add the garlic and cook, stirring frequently, for an additional minute.
  6. Stir in Worcestershire sauce and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Transfer ground beef mixture to prepared baking dish and spread evenly.
  8. Top with cream of mushroom soup, followed by green beans, followed by the cheddar cheese. Make sure to season the green bean layer.
  9. Finally, top with tater tots.
  10. Bake for 35-40 minutes or until the tater tots are golden brown and cheese is melted.
  11. Serve immediately.
