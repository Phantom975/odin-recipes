Tater Tot Casserole

Description

Tater tot casserole is a simple and fast meal for busy families. The biggest benefit this dish has is the versatility, so you can alter it as much as you need depending on what you have on hand. A large variety of vegetables can be used and a multitude of cream soups can be used to pull it together. Anybody who has a busy lifestyle should certainly keep ingredients on hand to be able to make this in a pinch.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb lean ground beef (the leaner the better)
  • 1/2 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 15 oz can of green beans, drained
  • 10.75 oz can condensed of cream of mushroom soup
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 cups frozen tater tots
  • salt and pepper to taste
