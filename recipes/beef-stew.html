Beef Stew

Description

Beef stew has always been a go to recipe as it's simple to prepare and can easily be made for any number of people. There are plenty of ways to prepare a beef stew you can always change what cut of meat you use and what vegetables. The best part about making any type of stew is the longer you cook it the better it tastes, so fire up your slow cooker and let it cook all afternoon.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp freshly ground pepper
  • 1 lb beef stewing meat, trimmed and cut into inch cubes
  • 5 tsps vegetable oil
  • 2 tbps red wine vinegar
  • 1 cup red wine
  • 3 1/2 cups beef broth, homemade or low-sodium canned
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 medium onion, peeled and chopped
  • 5 medium carrots, peeled and cut in 1/4-inch rounds
  • 2 large baking potatoes, peeled and cut into 3/4-inch cubes
  • 2 tsps salt
